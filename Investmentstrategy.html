Money for Minnows Investment Strategy

Kegonsa managed funds typically employ a Money for Minnows invest strategy

"Money for Minnows promotes new company creation. The Money for Minnows investment criteria includes having the expected investor return being company acquisition in less than five years. The primary success metric is investor return benchmarked to the seed/early stage venture capital national average return."
