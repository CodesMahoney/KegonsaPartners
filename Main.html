Welcome to Kegonsa Capital Partners

Kegonsa Capital Partners, LLC is a Wisconsin-based venture capital management firm.

Investing in People

Kegonsa Capital Partners was founded in 2004 to manage Wisconsin-based venture capital funds focusing on being the first $500K investor in entrepreneurial startups. The product is new, market size zero and the entrepreneur is passionate.

With KCP as the Badger Fund of Funds Wisconsin partner, the Badger Fund is the lead investor in six Wisconsin based VC funds managed by first time fund managers.
